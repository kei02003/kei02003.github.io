PM Automation Maturity

A lightweight framework for evaluating how much of a product management workflow should be automated, instrumented, or kept deliberately manual.

Status: Live

The model breaks work into repeatable operating loops: intake, prioritization, delivery coordination, stakeholder reporting, and retrospective analysis. Each loop can then be assessed for signal quality, decision risk, and automation readiness.

The practical goal is not to automate everything. It is to identify where automation improves speed and consistency without removing judgment from product work that still depends on context.
